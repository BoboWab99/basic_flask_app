My First Project

The Rosy Path

This was my first painting. I began working on it in January 2016 and finished it in march of the same year;

It was inspired by one of my visits to the scenic fields of Kiambu Country, on the northern side of the Aberdare slopes.

On one sunny morning, the fields were very clear, with flowers spanning miles unending. The scenery was beckoning; urging me to pick up my brush and recreate it.
